Digital Employability & Entrepreneurship Enhancement
Program (DEEP)



MKCL is offering series of job and career oriented courses under its “KLiC Brand” over a decade
in Maharashtra and a few other states. KLiC (क्लिक् / ક્લિક) stands for Knowledge Lit Careers. DEEP is MKCL’s
flagship Digital Employability & Entrepreneurship Enhancement Program (DEEP) for youth under which
several KLiC Certificate Courses in variety of digital domains are offered to aspiring Learners.
Normally 1 lac Learners take KLiC courses every year at MKCL’s Authorized Learning Centers (ALCs)
popularly called as KLiC Centers.



Almost all Learners who desire to get placed are given job offers through MKCL’s EASY Portal. EASY
stands for ‘Earning / Employment Assistance Services to Youth’.



DEEP Diploma is awarded after completing a sequence of 2 Mandatory Foundational KLiC Certificate Courses
followed by 1 or 2 Elective KLiC Certificate Courses in one or more Tracks totalling to 360 or more Learning
Hours to be completed by the Learner.
